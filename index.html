Our Story

Founded in 2010, Brew Haven began as a small coffee cart in downtown Portland. Our passion for quality coffee and community quickly grew into what is now a beloved local coffee shop.

We directly source our beans from sustainable farms and roast them in small batches to ensure maximum freshness and flavor. Every cup tells a story of craftsmanship and care.

Our baristas are trained in the art of coffee making, ensuring each beverage is prepared to perfection. We believe coffee is more than a drink - it's an experience.
